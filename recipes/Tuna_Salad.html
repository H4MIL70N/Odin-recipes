Avocado Tuna Salad

Description

Looking for a quick and easy tuna salad that's perfect for lunchtime? This avocado tuna salad is the one for you. It's packed with flavors and ingredients that will keep you full.

Ingredients

Here's what you'll need to make this tasty avocado tuna salad:

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 (5 ounce) cans flaked light tuna
  • ½ cup chopped red onion
  • ½ cup chopped celery
  • ¼cup chopped toasted walnuts
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise (Optional)
  • 1 teaspoon dried dill weed
  • ½ teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Direction

  1. Gather ingredients.

  2. Cut avocado in half, scoop out the flesh into a large bowl, and mash avocado with the back of a fork or potato masher.

  3. Add tuna, onion, celery, toasted walnuts, mayonnaise, dill, Dijon mustard, cumin, salt, and pepper; mix well.

  4. Enjoy!
